Our Principles : People, Planet, and Profit

People:

Product ingredients are made with the sustainability of nature in mind Using reusable packaging Initiating beauty empties digital takeback program with Waste4Change and Collaborating with environmental conservation organization like BOS Fondation and Lindungi Hutan.

Planet:

Developing community through RUBI to empower women in a sustainable manner Worked together with Indonesian community such as Torajamelo socio-brand who supports the development of women's lives in Mamasa, Lembata and Adonara.

Profit:

Collaborating with non profit organization like PUKA, local businesses that focus on crafting while providing creative media for the education of children with special needs. Fair trade system with our upstream to downstream partners. We make this effort to also make products at fair prices so that all sustainable activities that we carry out can also run sustainably.
